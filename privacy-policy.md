# Privacy Policy

**FreeSpend**
*Last updated: February 19, 2026*

---

## Overview

FreeSpend is a personal budgeting app for tracking discretionary spending. This policy describes how the App handles your information.

---

## Data Collection

The App uses Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage data. This may include:

- App open and session events
- Device type, OS version, and language
- General location (country/region, derived from IP address)
- Crash reports and performance data

This data is used solely to improve the App and is not used to identify you personally.

---

## Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Balance** | Your current discretionary spending balance (a numeric value) |
| **Last Updated** | A timestamp of the most recent balance change |

This data is stored using Apple's standard UserDefaults mechanism and shared between the main app and the home screen widget via an App Group. It never leaves your device.
